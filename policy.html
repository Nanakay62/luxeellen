Shipping Policy

At Ellen Luxe, we ensure your premium hair reaches you safely and swiftly.

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 2-3 business days.
  • Domestic Shipping: 3-5 business days via FedEx/DHL.
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days. Custom duties are the responsibility of the customer.
  • Tracking: You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped.

Returns & Exchanges

Due to the nature of our products and federal health regulations, all hair extensions are final sale.

However, we do offer exchanges under the following conditions:

  • The security seal or "gold tie" has NOT been broken or removed.
  • The hair has not been brushed, combed, washed, or installed.
  • Exchange requests must be made within 7 days of delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can the hair be bleached or dyed?

Yes, Ellen Luxe hair is 100% Virgin/Raw hair and can be lifted to 613. We recommend professional coloring to maintain hair integrity.

How long does the hair last?

With proper care, our bundles and units can last 2-3+ years.

How many bundles do I need for a full head?

For lengths 12"-18", we recommend 3 bundles. For 20" and above, we recommend 4 bundles for maximum fullness.
